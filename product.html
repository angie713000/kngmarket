The West Indian Potato, also known as Batata, is a sweet and starchy root vegetable widely used in Caribbean cooking. Its naturally sweet flavor and dense texture make it perfect for soups, stews, boiling, frying, and baking.

At K&G Market, we source our Batata fresh from trusted growers, ensuring you get the best quality product every time you shop with us.
