Frequently Asked Questions
Do you accept EBT / SNAP?
Yes! We proudly accept EBT and SNAP benefits. We also have an ATM available in-store for your convenience.
Can I place a special order for specific Caribbean products?
Absolutely! We love special orders. Contact us by phone or email with your request and we'll do our best to source it for you within 2–3 business days.
Do you offer pickup or delivery?
We currently offer free in-store pickup. Simply order online and your items will be ready within 2 hours. Home delivery coming soon!
How fresh are your products?
We hand-select every product daily from trusted growers to ensure exceptional freshness and quality. Our produce is restocked every morning before we open.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Cash, and EBT/SNAP. All major payment methods are welcome.
